Delete Your QuitVape Account

At QuitVape, you have full control over your data and account. If you no longer wish to use the app, you can request account deletion at any time. Once your account is deleted, all personal data associated with it will also be permanently removed within 90 days.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the QuitVape app.
  2. Go to Settings → Delete Account.
  3. Confirm deletion to permanently remove your account and associated data.

Alternative Method

If you are unable to access the app, you can request account deletion by emailing us at: quitvape.contact@gmail.com with the subject line “Delete My QuitVape Account”. Please include the email address linked to your account.

Data Retention

- Once your account is deleted, all user data will be permanently erased within 90 days.
- We may retain minimal information if required by law (e.g., financial transaction records).

For more details, please review our Privacy Policy and Terms of Use.
