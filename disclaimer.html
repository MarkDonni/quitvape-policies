Health & Safety Disclaimer

QuitVape provides general education about quitting nicotine and potential health changes after stopping vaping or smoking. It is not medical advice.

What this app does

  • Shares research-based timelines and tips for behavior change.
  • Offers motivation and tracking to support your quit attempt.
  • Provides general information that may help you talk with your clinician.

What this app does not do

  • It does not diagnose, treat, or prevent disease.
  • It does not replace care from a doctor, pharmacist, or other qualified professional.
  • It does not guarantee results. Timelines and outcomes vary widely between individuals.

Seek professional advice

Always consult a qualified health professional about symptoms, medications, or medical decisions. In an emergency, call your local emergency number immediately.

Medication and product information

Talk to a clinician before starting, stopping, or changing any medication, supplement, or therapy. Product references are informational only and not endorsements.

Sources and variability

Timelines in the app are based on public health and clinical sources. They describe typical patterns, not promises. Factors like age, history of use, conditions, and environment can change outcomes.

Data and privacy

Your in-app entries are for personal tracking only. They should not be used as a medical record.
